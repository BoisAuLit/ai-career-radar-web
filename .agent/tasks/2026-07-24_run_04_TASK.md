## Objective

lint layer that can measure and fail structural evidence regressions
**without rewriting output, invoking an LLM, or automatically
retrying generation**.

The validator design must cover:

1. Required Evidence Appendix.
2. At least 5 parser-recognized `Evidence quote:` lines.
3. One supporting citation in each of the 5 ranked gaps.
4. Body-to-Appendix consistency.
5. Clear separation from quote-integrity R1 / R2 matching.
6. Future baseline and regression-harness integration boundaries.

**Inspection / design only. Cost $0.**
