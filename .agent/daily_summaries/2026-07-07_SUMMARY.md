### Audit verdict

**Promising but needs targeted improvement.**

- Architecture is worth keeping. Evidence-
  grounded report design, hallucination
  guardrails, 3-judge eval concept, and
  the conservative May 2026 corpus posture
  all check out on paper.
- The product should NOT pivot away from
  the current report architecture yet.
- The next work should target **concrete
  reliability / UX risks in real generated
  reports** — not prompt rewrites, not
  model swaps, not architecture changes.

### Top 5 risks (with citations)

1. **Silent stream truncation can make
   incomplete reports appear complete.**
   `src/app/page.tsx:542-550` treats
   `reader.read().done === true` as
   success — a mid-stream Anthropic
   network wobble just paints "done" over
   a half-report. **Highest severity for
   trust.**
2. **Quote attribution is currently
   largely model-honor-based.**
   `src/lib/prompts.ts:97-98` asks for
   real JD quotes but nothing substring-
   verifies them against the 5 supplied
   JD bodies; the groundedness judge
   (`src/lib/eval-report.ts:94-118`)
   checks claim classes, not substrings.
3. **Sample-report presentation is
   visually richer than the real streamed
   markdown report.** Colored gap
   medallions + hand-drawn 7-day plan
   grid at
   `src/app/sample-report/page.tsx:115-337`
   vs. plain `<ReactMarkdown>` at
   `src/app/page.tsx:1284-1286`.
4. **Empty or near-empty PDF extraction
   can silently populate the résumé
   field.** `unpdf` returns near-empty
   text on scanned PDFs; frontend
   (`src/app/page.tsx:486-502`) blindly
   `setResume(data.text)` on any 2xx.
5. **Eval is not inline by default,
   while visible trust metrics may feel
   stronger than runtime proof.** Eval
   requires clicking `📊 Eval this
   report` (`src/app/page.tsx:1306-1315`);
   homepage hero stats
   (`src/app/page.tsx:1519-1553`) are
   hardcoded aggregate numbers.

### Default recommendation

**Candidate 1 — stream-complete sentinel
+ "report may be incomplete" banner.**

Reason:
- **Highest severity** (fixes risk #1).
- **Narrowest high-value fix.**
- **Affects every generated report**, not
  just users who click eval.
- **Improves trust without changing
  prompts or report content.**

### Candidate 1 implementation nuance

- A page-only change may not be enough
  to reliably know whether the
  server-side stream completed.
- Recommended future implementation
  likely touches:
  - `src/app/api/generate-report/route.ts`
  - `src/app/page.tsx`

  sentinel** (e.g. a distinctive
  terminator token appended after
  `streamText` finishes on the server).
- **Client should detect** whether the

- If the sentinel is missing, show a
  **conservative incomplete-report

- **Do NOT ask the model to emit the

  the model output, not by asking the
