### Structural invariants

- exact `## Evidence Appendix` heading is **mandatory**
- at least **5** recognized `Evidence quote:` lines are **mandatory**
- each of the 5 ranked gap items **must** contain a supporting `jd_id` citation
- exact citation form: `Evidence quote: "TEXT" — Company, jd_XXXXXX.`
- Appendix uses **one canonical parser-compatible row format**
- quote difficulty may **not** cause omission

### Faithfulness invariants

- each quote is one **exact contiguous source substring**
- no ellipsis bridging
- no grammar repair
- no paraphrase
- no added, removed, or altered words
- interpretation stays **outside** quotation marks
- short incomplete source fragments are valid **and preferred**

### Minimum count

- **5**
- matches 5 ranked gaps
- matches healthy controlled A/B reports
- do NOT raise above 5 in first implementation
- do NOT require `jd_000201` itself to appear
- if `jd_000201` appears, its quote MUST be exact
- 5 citations must provide support across the 5 ranked gaps, not merely be concentrated in one section

### Appendix contract

- **exact heading**: `## Evidence Appendix`
- **required placement**: after highest-leverage next-action section
- include each cited `jd_id`, company, and title
- use **one canonical parser-compatible row format**
- de-duplicate repeated jobs
- uncited entries remain allowed but telemetered
- omission is **invalid**
- shorter fragment is the fallback

### Instruction hierarchy

1. mandatory structure
2. quote exactness
3. short-fragment fallback
4. final checklist

### Self-check

**First**:

- 5 recognized evidence lines exist
- each top-5 gap contains supporting citation
- Appendix exists

**Second**:

- every quote is exact and contiguous
- no ellipsis bridging
- no grammar repair or paraphrase

**Third**:

- every `jd_id` exists
- Appendix / body relationship is valid

### Example guidance

- one compact synthetic positive example
- at most **2** compact synthetic negative examples
- positive example demonstrates:
  - short exact fragment
  - interpretation outside quote
  - correct company / `jd_id` suffix
  - Appendix representation
- negative examples cover:
  - ellipsis bridging
  - grammar repair or total omission
- no proprietary source content
- avoid repetitive negative instructions
