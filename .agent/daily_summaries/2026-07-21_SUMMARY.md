### Recommended thresholds

- **GREEN**:
  - Evidence Appendix present when required
  - all quoted spans found verbatim in matched source JD `body`
  - citation / source `id` present
  - company and role metadata align
  - no duplicate evidence counted as independent support
- **AMBER**:
  - quote matches only after whitespace/punctuation normalization
  - non-critical metadata missing but source `id` resolves
  - evidence item weakly connected to claim
  - duplicate evidence exists but does not change conclusion
- **RED**:
  - fabricated quote
  - quote not found in source `body`
  - wrong company attribution
  - wrong role/job attribution
  - quote from one role used to support another role
  - Evidence Appendix missing when report claims JD evidence
  - report claim materially stronger than evidence supports
  - source `id` cannot be resolved
